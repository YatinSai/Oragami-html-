Intersting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  • Chameleons mainly change color to communicate
  • Unlike lizards chameleons can't re-grow their tails

Intersting Facts About Camels

  • Camels humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour, the same as a racehorse
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day

Intersting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intersting Facts About Bears

  • Bears are omnivores
  • Bears hibernate during winter
  • Bears can reach a weight of between 150 and 370 kilogrammes

Intersting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment
  • Pandas eyes are different to normal bears
  • Pandas have five fingers

Intersting Facts About Flying Cicadas

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall
  • Cicadas have red-orange eyes
  • Cicadas are called 'tiny violens'
